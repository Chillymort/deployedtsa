Choripan Grill

About Us

Choripan Grill is dedicated to supported local farmers. We realize the importance of
agricultural sustainability, and we commit ourselves to directly sourcing from farms that prioritize
sustainable farming practices.


By sourcing from local farms, we eliminate the need for long transportation, which ultimately
leads to waste in our atmosphere.


Our restaurant is fully vegetarian, reducing gas emissions, soil and water pollution, and resource
intensive practices cattle farming induces. We compost organic food waste, reducing the need for
chemical fertilizers.


At Choripan Grill, we hope to make a real impact on reducing the carbon footprint of humanity,
while also providing incredible, vegetarian South American food to customers.
